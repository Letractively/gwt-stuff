GWT-Stuff-EventList.jar - The EventList lib from the GWT-Stuff library.

GWT-Stuff-EventList.jar contails all EventList modules provided by the
GWT-Stuff project.

Dependencies: none, unless you use PropertyChangeEventList

Module XML:

<inherits name="org.mcarthur.sandy.gwt.event.list.EventList"/> :
    This inherits the EventList lib for use in your project.

<inherits name="org.mcarthur.sandy.gwt.event.list.property.PropertyChangeEventList"/> :
    This inherts the PropertyChangeEventList for use in your project.
    This module depends on GWT-Stuff-PropertyChange.
